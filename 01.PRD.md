# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

Project Name: Social Intelligence Platform

Version: 1.0

Status: Approved

## Product Vision

Membangun platform backend Social Intelligence berbasis AI untuk mengumpulkan, memproses, menganalisis, dan menghasilkan insight dari media sosial, berita, forum, dan sumber publik lainnya.

Platform bersifat API First dan AI First.

## Goals

* Monitoring Brand
* Monitoring Public Sentiment
* Monitoring Political Issues
* Competitor Analysis
* Trend Intelligence
* Executive Insight Generation

## Target Users

* Marketing Team
* PR Team
* Government Agency
* Research Team
* Political Analyst
